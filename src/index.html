Top 3 must visits

Saif ul malook

Saif ul Malook Lake, located in the northern Pakistan's Kaghan Valley, captivates with its crystal-clear waters, surrounded by majestic mountains, folklore, and natural beauty.

Skardu

Skardu, Pakistan, nestled in the Karakoram Range, boasts breathtaking landscapes, including towering peaks, serene lakes, and lush valleys, making it a paradise for adventurers and nature lovers alike.

Balakot

Balakot, Pakistan, situated in the scenic Kaghan Valley, is known for its lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and as a gateway to popular tourist destinations in the Himalayas.
